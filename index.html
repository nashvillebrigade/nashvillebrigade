Who

The Nashville Civic Brigade provides citizens an opportunity to do what is most quintessentially Tennessean: roll up our sleeves, get involved and work together to improve our community. Software developers, designers, entrepreneurs, and other volunteers partner to build new solutions using publicly-released data and code. We work in partnership with local government agencies and meet once a month to learn more about local civic hacking.
